Delete Your Account

You can delete your Pawvo account and all associated data at any time. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile (name and email address)
  • All pet profiles and photos you've uploaded
  • All conversation transcripts and session history
  • Your pet's personality data and voice settings
  • Any paired device associations

What is retained

  • Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics that cannot be linked back to you
  • Records required by law or to resolve ongoing disputes (if any)

How to delete your account

Option 1 — In the app (fastest):

  1. Open the Pawvo app and sign in
  2. Tap the Settings icon in the top right
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion — your account is removed immediately

Option 2 — By email (if you no longer have access to the app):

Email a deletion request
Include the email address associated with your Pawvo account. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 business days.
