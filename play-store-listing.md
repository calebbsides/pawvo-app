## Full description (≤ 4000 chars)

Ever wonder what your pet is actually trying to tell you?

Pawvo listens to your pet's sounds and uses AI to translate them into words — giving your dog, cat, or other animal a unique personality and voice that grows and evolves the more you use it.

**How it works**
Pair the Pawvo app with a Wear OS smartwatch worn by your pet. The watch captures your pet's vocalizations and sends them to the Pawvo AI pipeline, which transcribes the sounds, generates a personality-matched response, and speaks it back in your pet's own synthesized voice — all in seconds.

**Key features**
• Live conversation transcript — watch your pet's words appear in real time on your phone
• Evolving personality — your pet's AI voice learns from every session and develops a consistent character over time
• Multiple pets — create profiles for each of your animals, each with their own unique voice and personality
• Personality history — browse how your pet's personality traits have evolved across sessions
• Session library — save and revisit past conversations
• Feedback system — rate your pet's responses to help the AI improve
• Wear OS companion app — runs directly on the watch with voice-activity detection so it only transmits when your pet makes a sound

**Supported pets**
Dogs, cats, and other animals — Pawvo works with any pet that vocalizes.

**Privacy**
Audio clips are processed securely by our AI pipeline and are not stored permanently. Location data is used only for environmental context (indoor vs. outdoor) and is never sold or used for advertising. See our full privacy policy for details.

**Requirements**
• Android 9 (API 28) or higher
• A paired Wear OS watch (API 30 / Wear OS 3.0 or higher) for live session capture
• Internet connection
