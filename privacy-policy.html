Pawvo Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Pawvo ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Pawvo mobile application (the "App"). This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: your name and email address when you register.
  • Pet profile information: pet name, species, breed, age, and an optional photo you upload.
  • Audio recordings: short audio clips of your voice, captured by your paired Wear OS device when you speak to your pet. These clips are transmitted to our servers for AI-powered speech-to-text processing and are stored to generate conversation transcripts.
  • Location data: approximate GPS coordinates and accelerometer readings from your paired Wear OS device. Location is used solely to provide environmental context (e.g., indoor vs. outdoor) to the AI pipeline and is not used for advertising or sold to third parties.
  • Device identifiers: a device pairing ID used to associate your Wear OS device with your account.
  • Usage data: conversation session timestamps, transcript history, and in-app feedback ratings you submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and provide the core App features: processing your speech through our AI pipeline to generate a response in your pet's voice.
  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session securely via AWS Cognito.
  • To store your pet profiles, personality data, and conversation transcripts in our database.
  • To send you push notifications related to your account (e.g., session summaries) via Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • To improve our AI models and service quality using aggregated, de-identified data.

3. Information Shared with Third Parties

We share data with the following service providers solely to operate the App. They are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

  • Groq, Inc. — audio clips and text are sent to Groq's API for speech-to-text transcription and AI language model inference. Audio data leaves our AWS infrastructure and is processed by Groq. See Groq's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — we use AWS Cognito (authentication), RDS PostgreSQL (database), S3 (audio and photo storage), Polly (text-to-speech), SES (email), Lambda, and API Gateway. Data is stored in the us-east-1 region. See AWS Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase — we use Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver push notifications. See Google Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

4. Data Retention

  • Account and pet profile data is retained until you delete your account.
  • Conversation transcripts are retained for 90 days by default. You can delete individual sessions at any time from within the App.
  • Audio clips are processed in near-real-time and are not stored permanently on our servers beyond what is required for transcript generation.

5. Data Security

All data is transmitted over TLS (HTTPS/WSS). Data at rest in S3 is encrypted using server-side encryption (SSE-S3). Passwords are never stored by us — authentication is handled by AWS Cognito. Refresh tokens on your device are protected by Android Keystore-backed biometric encryption.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access or correct your account information within the App.
  • Delete individual conversation sessions within the App.
  • Delete your entire account and all associated data by contacting us at the address below.
  • Opt out of push notifications in your device settings.

If you are in the EU/EEA or UK, you may also have rights under GDPR/UK GDPR to data portability, restriction of processing, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy here and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
