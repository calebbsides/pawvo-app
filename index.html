Features

Everything your pet
has been trying to tell you

A complete system — phone app, Wear OS companion, and an AI that gets to know your pet.

🎙️

Real-time voice translation

Your pet's vocalizations are captured by a Wear OS watch, transcribed by AI, and translated into natural language responses in seconds.

🧠

Evolving personality

The AI learns from every session. Your pet develops a consistent character — with wit, quirks, and a way of speaking that's uniquely theirs.

📱

Live conversation transcript

Watch your pet's words appear on your phone in real time. Save and revisit past conversations to remember the best moments.

⌚

Wear OS companion

A smartwatch worn by your pet captures sounds and sensor data. Smart voice-activity detection means it only fires when your pet vocalizes.

🐕🐈

Multiple pets

Add as many pets as you like — each with their own profile, voice, and personality that develops independently over time.

🔒

Private by design

Audio is processed securely and not stored permanently. Location data is used only for environmental context and is never sold.

How it works

From bark to sentence
in seconds

A seamless pipeline runs in the background — you just talk to your pet.

01

Pet makes a sound

The Wear OS watch detects voice activity and captures the audio clip. Only real vocalizations trigger a capture — no background noise.

02

AI transcribes and understands

Audio is transcribed using speech-to-text AI, combined with sensor context like movement and location to understand the situation.

03

Personality generates a response

A language model — shaped by your pet's unique personality profile — generates a response in your pet's own voice and style.

04

Spoken in your pet's voice

The response is synthesized using Amazon Polly in a voice matched to your pet and played back through your phone's speaker.
